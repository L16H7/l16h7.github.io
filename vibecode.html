I am in love with vibecoding

Apr 29, 2025

Why do we code? For me, I love solving problems and it just happens that solving problems with code pays money.

I see "vibecoding" as an expression of your imagination rather than solving software problems.

I have been using v0dev to vibecode the entire frontend for a week and it's been going well. I never enjoy centering a div and making the app beautiful. I am bad at it and don't want to work on it.

I believe vibecoding will solve more problems in most tech-related jobs and create problems as well. e.g., refactoring the mess I vibecoded.

I do not believe AI will solve all the problems because we, humans, are going to create more complex problems. That's what we have done since the dawn of humanity. :3

If AI can solve trivial problems, that will give us time to work on complex problems. That's the future I see, the future where AI asks for help, not the other way around.
